About Us

Mang Inasal is a popular Filipino fast food chain known for its grilled chicken. Founded in 2003, we take pride in offering delicious, freshly grilled dishes with a unique taste.

Our commitment is to provide great-tasting food and excellent service in a family-friendly environment. Join us for a memorable dining experience!
